About Us

This website, themed around the month of Ramadan, was created to provide easier access for Muslims to obtain relevant information about fasting, prayer schedules, iftar, sahur, and other related religious knowledge. In welcoming this holy month, the website not only offers practical information but also aims to create a digital community where people can share experiences and inspiration. It is hoped that this platform will strengthen the spirit of worship, foster solidarity, and enhance the sense of togetherness among Muslims.
