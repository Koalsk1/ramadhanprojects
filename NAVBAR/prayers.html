Islamic Prayers

A collection of authentic prayers and supplications

Morning Supplication

أَصْبَحْنَا وَأَصْبَحَ الْمُلْكُ لِلَّهِ، وَالْحَمْدُ لِلَّهِ، لَا إِلَهَ إِلَّا اللَّهُ وَحْدَهُ لَا شَرِيكَ لَهُ
We have reached the morning and kingship belongs to Allah, praise is to Allah. None has the right to be worshipped except Allah, alone, without any partner.
Morning

Evening Supplication

أَمْسَيْنَا وَأَمْسَى الْمُلْكُ لِلَّهِ، وَالْحَمْدُ لِلَّهِ، لَا إِلَهَ إِلَّا اللَّهُ وَحْدَهُ لَا شَرِيكَ لَهُ
We have reached the evening and kingship belongs to Allah, praise is to Allah. None has the right to be worshipped except Allah, alone, without any partner.
Evening

Before Sleep

بِاسْمِكَ رَبِّي وَضَعْتُ جَنْبِي، وَبِكَ أَرْفَعُهُ، إِنْ أَمْسَكْتَ نَفْسِي فَارْحَمْهَا، وَإِنْ أَرْسَلْتَهَا فَاحْفَظْهَا بِمَا تَحْفَظُ بِهِ عِبَادَكَ الصَّالِحِينَ
In Your name, my Lord, I lay down, and in Your name I rise. If You should take my soul, then have mercy upon it, and if You should return my soul, then protect it as You protect Your righteous servants.
Before Sleep
